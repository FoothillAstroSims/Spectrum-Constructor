Eyeball view

This is demonstration of two views of a spectrum - the "by eye" view through a diffraction grating and a 1D spectrum plot (as from a spectrometer). This also illustrates the color each combination of wavelengths would look to the human eye.

The brightnesses for the emission lines are not based on calculations of gas temperature, density, and radiation field. They are approximations, tuned to approximate what is seen in the particular set of emission lamps currently used by the Foothill College Astronomy Department.
